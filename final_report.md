# Final Project Report

## Project Title
Survey Probability Analysis of Preferred Social Media App

## Objective
To analyze survey responses using probability concepts.

## Total Responses
60

## Frequency Distribution

| App | Frequency |
|------|-----------|
| Instagram | 28 |
| YouTube | 14 |
| TikTok | 12 |
| Facebook | 6 |

## Probability Distribution

| App | Probability |
|------|------------|
| Instagram | 0.4667 |
| YouTube | 0.2333 |
| TikTok | 0.2000 |
| Facebook | 0.1000 |

## Findings

- Instagram is the most selected application.
- Facebook is the least selected application.
- Daily users show a stronger preference toward Instagram.
- Probability distribution and simulation analysis show similar trends.

## Conclusion

The survey probability analysis successfully identified user preferences and trends. Instagram has the highest probability of selection and is therefore the most preferred platform among respondents.
